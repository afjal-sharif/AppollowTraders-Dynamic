📋 Step 1: Copy the HTML content

Open dist/index.html and copy ALL content

Or click the button below to see it (if you opened this file from the project):

The content should start with <!doctype html>

📋 Step 2: Go to Cloudflare KV and paste

Instructions:

  1. Go to dash.cloudflare.com
  2. Click Workers & Pages → KV
  3. Click on your DATA_STORE namespace
  4. Click "+ Add entry"
  5. Key: type APP_HTML
  6. Value: paste the HTML content from Step 1
  7. Click Save

📋 Step 3: Update worker.js

Instructions:

  1. Go to Workers & Pages
  2. Click your worker name
  3. Click "Edit Code"
  4. Select All (Ctrl+A) → Delete everything
  5. Copy ALL content of DEPLOY-worker.js and paste
  6. Click "Save and Deploy"
⚠️ Make sure your worker has these bindings in Settings:
• KV Namespace: DATA_STORE
• R2 Bucket: DOC_BUCKET
